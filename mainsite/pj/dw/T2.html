What is Type 2 Diabetes ?

If you have type 2 diabetes, your body does not use insulin properly.This is called insulin resistance. At first, your pancreas makes extra insulin to make up for it. But, over time it isn't able to keep up and can't make enough insulin to keep your blood glucose at normal levels. Adults, usually over the age of 40 have diabetes type 2. there is a greater risk in those who have poor diets and are overweight.
